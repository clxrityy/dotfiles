Look in-depth at the current files and directories (and their respective structure) in the working directory. Understand the purpose of each file and directory, and how they relate to each other. Identify any patterns or conventions used in the organization of the files and directories. Consider how the structure of the files and directories might impact the development process, and whether there are any potential improvements that could be made to enhance efficiency or maintainability.

Your goal is to gain a comprehensive understanding of the overall structure and organization, and to inform to the best of your ability how the files and directories work together to support the development process in relation to the user's intent/goals/queries.
